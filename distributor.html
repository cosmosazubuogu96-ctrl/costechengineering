The Opportunity

Why This Market. Why Now.

Africa's manufacturing sector is expanding rapidly. NAFDAC, KEBS, FDA Ghana, and regulatory bodies across the continent are enforcing stricter product coding and traceability requirements — creating urgent, consistent demand for TIJ coding solutions.

📈

Growing Regulatory Pressure

Manufacturers across Africa are legally required to print expiry dates, batch codes, and traceability marks on products. This is not optional — it is a compliance requirement driving consistent equipment demand.

🏭

Underserved Industrial Market

Food & beverage, pharma, FMCG, plastics, cables, and agro-processing industries in most African countries currently have limited access to quality, affordable TIJ coding solutions. The gap is your opportunity.

🔄

Recurring Revenue Model

Every printer sold requires regular ink cartridge replacements. As your customer base grows, so does your recurring consumables revenue — making this a sustainable, compounding business.

🤝

First-Mover Advantage

Positions are strictly one per country. The distributor who moves first secures the exclusive territory. Once a country is taken, it is closed to new applicants.
